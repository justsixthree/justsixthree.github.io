ABOUT ME

Family Owned and Operated 1949

The Chamberlin Real Estate School has been helping people launch a new real estate career for over 68 years. As a third generation family business, we are the oldest private real estate school in the state under the same family ownership. As a family business, we value and enjoy personal interaction with our students. We offer a unique “home-study” program, which includes online courses or textbook based courses with the benefit of “Personal Instructor” support and a LIVE 2-day weekend crash course. This combination of distance education with instructor support and live crash course has proven to be a superior method of preparing to pass the state salesperson and broker examination.
